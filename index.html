Five Simple Steps:

Putting a Foldabox gift box together couldn't be easier.

Step 1

All gift boxes are supplied flat for minimal storage

Step 2

Remove the corner papers to reveal the sticky surface

Step 3

Pinch the corners firmly together

Step 4

All gift boxes have concealed magnetic snap shut closures

Step 5

Tie a bow! We have gift boxes with fixed, changeable and without ribbon

All our gift boxes are this easy to assemble!
